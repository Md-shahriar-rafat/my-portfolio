P.01

Graph neural networks for small molecule–miRNA association in Alzheimer's disease

Lead — full pipeline, modeling, manuscript

Benchmarked graph neural networks against random-forest and network-based-inference (NBI) baselines for predicting small molecule–miRNA associations on the SmiRN-AD network. Built the pipeline end to end: GEO expression preprocessing (GSE48552), graph construction, and PyTorch Geometric models, with a leakage-safe message-passing scheme and controlled negative sampling.

Leakage-safe GNN outperformed both baselines. Adding pathway/context features gave no significant lift (p = 0.39) — framed as an equivalence result, not buried. Degree-bias stayed low across methods.

P.02

Integrated multi-omics prognostic model for hepatocellular carcinoma

Sole author — full R pipeline · public code

Built a multi-omics survival model for liver cancer (HCC) on TCGA-LIHC, fusing a 450K DNA-methylation array with RNA-seq. The pipeline pairs and normalizes IDATs in minfi, runs probe QC and KNN imputation, then applies univariate and LASSO-penalized Cox regression to derive a 9-CpG methylation signature and a 6-gene expression signature, combined into one integrated patient risk score.

Validated with 10-fold cross-validation and 1,000-iteration bootstrap resampling. Reports time-dependent ROC, Kaplan–Meier survival separation, and GO enrichment on the gene signature. Full pipeline is public and re-runnable.
